# Ghost Customer 

### Your customers, before they exist.

**Ghost Customer** is an AI-powered virtual focus group that lets founders and product teams validate ideas before writing a single line of code. Describe your product, generate a panel of realistic AI customer personas, and ask them anything — pricing reactions, feature feedback, adoption intent. Your panel disagrees, debates, and reacts to your actual copy, giving you the kind of insight that used to cost $10,000 and two weeks. Now it takes two minutes.

🔗 **Live app:** [ghostcustomer-ai.lovable.app](https://ghostcustomer-ai.lovable.app)

---

## The problem

Most founders skip customer validation entirely. Real focus groups are expensive ($5k–$20k), slow (weeks to recruit and run), and hard to access at the early stage when decisions matter most. The result: teams build the wrong thing, then wonder why nobody shows up.

Ghost Customer eliminates that gap.

---

## What it does

**1. Generate your panel**
Describe your product and target customer in plain language. Ghost Customer generates 4 distinct AI personas — each with a name, backstory, goals, frustrations, and a personality archetype (Skeptic, Early Adopter, Budget-Conscious, Loyalist). These aren't demographic clichés — they're opinionated, specific, and designed to surprise you.

**2. Ask them anything**
Type a question into the shared focus group chat. All 4 personas respond in their own voice, from their own perspective. Ask about pricing, onboarding, features, positioning — anything a real customer interview would surface.

**3. Panel Pulse — conflict mode**
After every round of responses, a "Panel Pulse" summary automatically appears. It shows where the panel agrees, where they disagree, and a one-line synthesis of overall sentiment. This is what real focus groups surface: not consensus, but the shape of opinion.

**4. React to My Copy**
Toggle to "React to My Copy" mode and paste your actual landing page headline, pricing table, or feature description. The panel reacts to the real text — not an abstraction. This is the fastest way to know if your copy lands before you spend money on ads.

### Why this idea

Real focus groups are inaccessible to most early-stage builders. Ghost Customer was built on the belief that every founder deserves customer insight before they commit — not after they've already shipped the wrong thing. The "panel" framing is deliberate: this isn't a chatbot, it's a room full of people who disagree with each other, and that friction is where the insight lives.

### What I learned shipping it

- AI builders like Lovable collapse the distance between idea and deployed product dramatically — the hard part is product thinking, not the code
- Persona quality is everything: generic demographics produce generic answers; specific life contexts produce surprising ones
- The "conflict" between personas is more valuable than any single response — synthesis is where the real insight emerges
- Shipping fast forces prioritization in a way that slow builds never do
